Fitness Center BIM Model

Single-story commercial fitness center modeled in Autodesk Revit 2026 with full HVAC system design and BIM coordination

Project Overview

Designed and modeled a single-story commercial fitness center in Autodesk Revit 2026, encompassing full architectural and mechanical systems for a 3,881 SF facility located in Chicago, Illinois. Engineering calculations were developed referencing ASHRAE 62.1 and 90.1 standards, and clash detection was performed in Navisworks Manage.

Architectural Design

Developed a complete architectural floor plan including a training area, men's and women's locker rooms, reception, office, and mechanical room. Applied finish materials and equipment families throughout the model including gym equipment, locker room fixtures, and office furniture.

HVAC System Design

Designed a complete HVAC system serving six zones including an air handling unit, supply ductwork, return ductwork, locker room exhaust system, and outdoor air intake. Diffuser placement and duct sizing were determined through engineering calculations referencing ASHRAE 62.1 and 90.1 standards.

Engineering Calculations

Developed a comprehensive HVAC calculation package including:
  • Room-by-room airflow requirements using ACH methodology
  • Duct sizing with velocity verification against ASHRAE limits
  • Outdoor air compliance verification per ASHRAE 62.1
  • Plumbing fixture unit calculations per IPC standards

BIM Coordination

Exported the Revit model to Navisworks Manage and performed clash detection between HVAC and architectural elements. Identified and reviewed 35 clashes, categorizing duct wall penetrations and diffuser installations as intentional design elements requiring field sleeve coordination.
